Why choose us

Erisa Trust Company is a chartered financial institution regulated by the State of New Mexico and a member of the Better Business Bureau of New Mexico and Southern Colorado. Our executives are members of the International Foundation of Employee Benefit Plans and the Society of Human Resources Management.

Erisa Trust Company maintains professional liability insurance and cyber-crime and digital-security insurances to protect our clients.

Erisa Trust Company has partnered with several organizations to provide you with the best possible service.

  • Healthcare Bank - custodial service as well as optional investment in selected mutual funds
  • Bancorp - debit card issuance and processing services
  • Charles Schwab - Brokerage Account Services
  • WEX Health - cloud-based IT system and mobile applications
